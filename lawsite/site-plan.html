About Us

We provide you with a variety of services here at Will, Powers, and L'Estat. Let us help you create the perfect plan tailor made to your needs.
Wills - Wills are how you tell your chosen executor what you want done with your assets once you pass.
Power of Attorney & Living Wills - The Power of Attorney document gives someone you trust the ability to make medical decisions should you be unable to. Living Wills also give a trusted person the power to enact your end-of-life wishes.
Estates - After a loved one has passed, our attorneys can help you enact their will and settle their affairs.
Nursing Homes - We want you to have the best care possible and will work with you to find a care facility that fits your needs. Our attorneys are always ready to answer your questions and ease your anxieties about taking the next step.

Meet Our Attorneys

Samantha Will is the head attorney of the Law Firm of Will, Powers, and L'Estat and has been practicing law for over 30 years! She graduated with honors from Harvard University and has gone on to get her doctorate in elder law. She primarily helps clients with their plans for to live a happy, comfortable life for the rest of their days.
Richard Powers will be the attorney you'll be working with to write up the majority of your documents. He graduated from Yale and specializes in the creation of documents such as Power of Attorney, Last Will and Testament, and Living Wills.
Todd L'Estat has been working at the Law Firm of Will, Powers, and L'Estat for 5 years and has been practicing elder law for a total of 15 years. He has the most experience with working with clients to plan their estates and providing a stress free experience for their loved ones to fufill their wishes after death. L'Estat has graduated from Brigham Young University and plans to get go on to get his doctorate.
