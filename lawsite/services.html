ABOUT US

We provide you with a variety of services here at Will, Powers, and L'Estat. Let us help you creat the perfect plan tailor made to your needs.

WILLS

Wills are how you tell your chosen executor what you want done with your assets once you pass.

POWER OF ATTORNEY & LIVING WILLS

The Power of Attorney document gives someone you trust the ability to make medical decisions should you be unable to. Living Wills also give a trusted person the power to enact your end-of-life wishes.

ESTATES

After a loved one has passed, our attorneys can help you enact their will and settle their affairs.

NURSING HOMES

We want you to have the best care possible and will work with you to find a care facility that fits your needs. Our attorneys are always ready to answer your questions and ease your anxieties aboiut taking the next step.
